CP1406 Assignment - 2 Project plan - FRIAS

Mission Statement

Our mission is to develop a professional website for FRIAS focussing on their fashion business and their expansion into the caf'e and fashion shows to help them grow in their business.

or

Our mission is to develop a fully functional website with a professional design for FRIAS that empowers their online presence and help them expand their business globally.

Success Evaluation

The site will have a good design with easy navigation. Call to action buttons can be seen through out the site which help the user understand what they are looking for. Balance in colors and images and unity in design will be followed.

Target Audience

The website aims at the age group of 4-12. The website color and font will be chosen accordingly to comply with the age group. Children in this age group dont usally have full knowledge about computers or how to navigate through a website, so most of the shopping would be done by the adults for their children, the site will have that professional design which showcases the companies eminence.

Content

Home Page - Will have good images and few lines of description about the company. Some announcements about the cafe and fashion shows will aslo be seen on the home page.

Products - This page will have all the products listed.

Cafe - This page will show the information regarding the opening date and location of the cafe and all the items and promotions that will be available.

Events - Information about the fashion shows and upcoming events will be shown here.

Services - This page will show information about the delivery date and shipping costs and other services the company provides.

Order now - This page will help the user order the items they want.

Contact - Will show the conatct info and a chat now button for the user to chat with the customer services representatives.

About us - All the info about the company can be found here. This page will also contain the FAQ's.

Feedback - This page is for the user to give feedback regarding any complaints or suggestions that the user has.
